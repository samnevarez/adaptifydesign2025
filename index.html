From 2010 to 2022, my experience in web design and development bloomed into a fulfilling career and passion. As a hybrid designer, these opportunities lead me to bridge efforts between design and development to achieve a delightful user experience with a deep understanding of front-end technologies. In addition, I enjoy backpacking, kettlebell training, playing, producing film scores, and the existential pleasures of eating tacos too.

Why the Mayan pyramid?

The photo of the pyramid above was taken when I visited Chichén Itzá in Mexico's Yucatán. I was very intrigued by Mayan architecture. They incorporated mathematics and spatial awareness with their structures. The shadows on these structures helped the Mayans determine the proper time to cultivate crops. At one point, these ruins were the central point for gathering and celebrating annual traditions. Today, the ruins demonstrate what it means to design methodically and with intention. That trip inspired me to design and code my portfolio to resemble an ancient time. I designed my own icons and animated background effects of the pyramid to personalize my website in remembrance of that amazing trip.

TEAM PRINCIPLES

Leadership

Obtain a strong understanding of the mission's “why?” Communicate that back down the ladder to subordinate leadership. Establish trust and ultimately allow people to do their jobs.

Never put yourself in a position where you take from those around you.

Communication

Resist isolation and let each other know what each team/person is doing.

Confidence / Ability

Trust in yourself and lean on your colleagues for support.
