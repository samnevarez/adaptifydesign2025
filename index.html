TEAM PRINCIPLES

Leadership

Obtain a strong understanding of the mission's “why?” Communicate that back down the ladder to subordinate leadership. Establish trust and ultimately allow people to do their jobs.

Never put yourself in a position where you take from those around you.

Communication

Resist isolation and let each other know what each team/person is doing.

Confidence / Ability

Trust in yourself and lean on your colleagues for support.
