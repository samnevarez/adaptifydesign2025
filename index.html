TEAM PRINCIPLES

Never put yourself in a position where you take from those around you.

Leadership

Lead by example. Stay grounded in curiosity, integrity, and the craft of design. Model the mindset you want to see across the team.

Empowerment

Give your team the space to own their work. Trust their process, champion their ideas, and create safe space for iteration and growth.

Adaptability

Embrace change. Agile environments demand flexibility—whether that’s pivoting on direction or iterating on systems. Resilience is a muscle.

Vision and Strategy

Define a north star. Align design decisions with business outcomes, and make sure every team member understands the "why" behind the work.

Cross-functional Collaboration

Bridge the gap between design, development, product, and marketing. Strong systems emerge when all voices are heard early and often.

Clear Communication

Transparency builds trust. Encourage honest conversations, early feedback, and regular alignment across tools, teams, and timelines.

Mentorship and Growth

Invest in your team. Offer feedback loops, stretch opportunities, and coaching that helps people level up—both as designers and humans.
